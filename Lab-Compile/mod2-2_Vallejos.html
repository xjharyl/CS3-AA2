Ponyo

In the tale of "Ponyo," a goldfish princess named Ponyo becomes enchanted by the surface world and befriends a human boy named Sosuke. Her strong desire to become human causes magical chaos when she escapes her kingdom, spilling elixirs that threaten Sosuke's village, leading to a magical and heartwarming adventure.
